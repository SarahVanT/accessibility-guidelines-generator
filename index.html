About WCAG

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) are guidelines designed to make the web accessible to everyone, including people with various disabilities. This Accessibility Guidelines Generator provides easy-to-follow guidance for improving web content accessibility. Just select a category from the options provided, click the 'Generate' button to submit your choice, and the tool will offer practical tips aligned with WCAG standards.

For more detailed information on WCAG, please visit the official WCAG guidelines.
